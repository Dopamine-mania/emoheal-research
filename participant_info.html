Participant Information Sheet

参与者信息表

Study Title: EmoHeal: An Experiential Study of an AI System for Personalized Therapeutic Video Generation
Ethics Reference: QMERC20.565.DSEECS25.045

What is this study about? / 研究目的是什么？

This research investigates how artificial intelligence can create personalized therapeutic experiences by analyzing your emotional state through text and recommending appropriate music and visual content. We aim to understand the effectiveness and user experience of such AI-driven therapeutic systems.

本研究调查人工智能如何通过分析文本中的情感状态来创建个性化治疗体验，并推荐合适的音乐和视觉内容。我们旨在了解此类AI驱动治疗系统的有效性和用户体验。

Who can participate? / 谁可以参与？

  • Adults aged 18 and over / 18岁及以上成年人
  • Able to understand and provide informed consent / 能够理解并提供知情同意
  • Comfortable using digital interfaces / 能够使用数字界面
  • No severe mental health conditions requiring immediate professional intervention / 没有需要立即专业干预的严重心理健康状况

What will happen if I take part? / 参与后会发生什么？

Study Procedure / 研究程序:

  1. Emotional Input - You will describe your current emotional state in text / 情感输入 - 您将用文字描述当前的情感状态
  2. AI Analysis - The system will analyze your emotions using AI / AI分析 - 系统将使用AI分析您的情绪
  3. Music Recommendation - Personalized therapeutic music will be selected / 音乐推荐 - 将选择个性化的治疗音乐
  4. Therapeutic Experience - You will experience the recommended audio-visual content / 治疗体验 - 您将体验推荐的音视频内容
  5. Feedback - Complete a brief questionnaire about your experience / 反馈 - 完成关于您体验的简短问卷

Total Duration: Approximately 15-20 minutes / 总时长：约15-20分钟

What are the potential benefits and risks? / 潜在的好处和风险是什么？

Benefits / 好处:

  • Experience a novel AI-driven therapeutic system / 体验新颖的AI驱动治疗系统
  • Potential relaxation and stress relief / 可能的放松和压力缓解
  • Contribute to research advancing digital mental health tools / 为推进数字心理健康工具的研究做出贡献

Risks / 风险:

  • Minimal risk - This is a low-risk study / 最小风险 - 这是一项低风险研究
  • Some participants may experience mild emotional responses to music / 一些参与者可能对音乐产生轻微的情感反应
  • If you feel uncomfortable at any time, you can withdraw immediately / 如果您在任何时候感到不适，可以立即退出

Will my participation be confidential? / 我的参与会保密吗？

Yes, absolutely. All data will be:

  • Anonymized immediately upon collection / 收集后立即匿名化
  • Stored securely on university systems / 安全存储在大学系统中
  • Only accessible to the research team / 仅研究团队可访问
  • Used solely for research purposes / 仅用于研究目的
  • Retained until January 2026, then securely destroyed / 保留至2026年1月，然后安全销毁

Can I withdraw from the study? / 我可以退出研究吗？

Yes, at any time. Participation is entirely voluntary. You can:

  • Withdraw before, during, or after the session / 在会议之前、期间或之后退出
  • Withdraw without providing a reason / 无需提供理由即可退出
  • Request deletion of your data (if identifiable) / 请求删除您的数据（如果可识别）
  • Experience no penalty or disadvantage / 不会受到任何惩罚或不利影响
